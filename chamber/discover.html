Discover the Eastern Hills Area

Days since your last visit:

The Eastern Hills area is home to some of the best farmer's markets in the state!

Our museum's new art exhibit is now open and ready for visitors.

Local bakeries and restaurants offer plenty of fresh and authentic dining options in our area.

The state fair is held in our area each year, providing entertainment for families and friends.

With Eastern Hills Community College and the other highly rated public and private schools in our area, we offer great opportunities for education.

We are just a short drive from all kinds of hikes, rivers, and trails- which means plenty of options for outdoor activities.

The business market in the area is quickly growing, so now is a great time to bring your business here!

Indoor recreation in the Eastern Hills area includes swimming, rock climbing, bowling, movies and more!

We have a population of about 50,000- so there's plenty of people to know and places to go, but you get to keep the small-town feel.
